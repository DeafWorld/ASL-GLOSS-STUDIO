Ready to see how it works? Try an example below.

English 🍕
"Where's the pizza?"
Gloss 🤟
PIZZA WHERE (big eyes / searching face)
English 🐶
"My dog ate my homework."
Gloss 🤟
HOMEWORK PAPER GONE WHY? (whq) DOG EAT (shift eyes, guilty)
English 🌌
"Dream big, let your hands paint the universe."
Gloss 🤟
DREAM BIG+++ HANDS PAINT ACROSS-UNIVERSE (sweeping, expansive motion)
English 🎭
"To be or not to be, that is the question."
Gloss 🤟
EXIST / NOT-EXIST, QUESTION DEEP (serious, thoughtful face)
English ✨
"Be kind, the world is watching."
Gloss 🤟
BE KIND, WORLD EYES-ON-YOU (point outward, circling gaze)
